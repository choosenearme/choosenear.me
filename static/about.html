What is choosenear.me?

Choosenear.me is a location-based platform used to find nearby DonorsChoose projects. Born out of the DonorsChoose Hacking Education contest, choosenear.me aims to integrate foursquare location-data into DonorsChoose projects.

So how does it all work?

It’s simple! After clicking the “Connect to foursquare” link on our main page, you’ll see some of your recent checkins on the left side grouped by cities. Not only is this a great way to find DonorsChoose projects around places you visit, but it’s also a fun way to view your foursquare trends and history.

Once you find a project that looks interesting, click on its title within the map bubble and go donate!

But wait, there’s more! Beyond just showing you projects around your checkins, choosenear.me features an advanced recommendation-engine. Using a combination of psychic-powers and a bit of luck… hehe just kidding… by analyzing the type of venues you frequent on foursquare, we’re able to come up with a pretty good idea of the type of things you like. From there, if the choosenear.me engine thinks a project would be an amazingly good match, you’ll see the map marker highlighted in gold.

Watch for these special gold and foursquare-blue map markers when you see a foursquare venue checkin noting that it contains recommended projects!

Even if you have yet to jump on the foursquare train, you can still view and track active DonorsChoose projects around your current location right from our main page!
